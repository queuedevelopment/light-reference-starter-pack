Lighting Cheat Sheet

Use with the Light Reference web app. Practice changing one variable at a time.

Three-Point

  • Key: az 30–45°, el 20–30°
  • Fill level: 0.2–0.35
  • Rim: opposite side, el 10–15°

Split

  • Key: az ≈ 90°, el 10–20°
  • Fill: 0–0.1 (optional)
  • Rim: subtle, to recover edge

Silhouette

  • Key off; Rim from behind
  • Watch interior values; avoid noise
  • Use bigger shapes

Temperature Study

  • Warm key (4200K), cool fill (8000K)
  • Invert and compare materials
  • Record frames
